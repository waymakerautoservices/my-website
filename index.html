About Waymaker

Waymaker Auto Services was founded by Steven Sudar after more than a decade working inside the dealership industry. Steven has worked in multiple roles including mechanic and salesperson, giving him firsthand knowledge of how dealerships structure deals, warranties, financing, and negotiations. During those years he saw how often buyers were rushed, misled, or left confused about what they were signing. Our mission is to bring honesty and clarity to the car buying process. As it says in John 14:6, Jesus said, “I am the way, the truth, and the life.” That commitment to truth guides how we serve every client.
