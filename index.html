About Waymaker

Waymaker Auto Services was founded by Steven Sudar after more than a decade working inside the dealership industry. Steven has worked as both a mechanic and a salesperson, giving him firsthand knowledge of how dealerships structure deals, warranties, financing, and negotiations. After seeing countless buyers rushed into confusing contracts and hidden fees, he founded Waymaker to bring honesty and clarity to the process. As it says in John 14:6, Jesus said, “I am the way, the truth, and the life.” That commitment to truth guides how we serve every client.
